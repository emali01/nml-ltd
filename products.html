Dolomite Powder

Our premium dolomite powder is manufactured from high-grade dolomite stones through a series of crushing, grinding, and classification processes. It is characterized by its high whiteness, purity, and consistent particle size distribution.

Chemical Formula CaMg(CO3)2
CaO Content 30-32%
MgO Content 19-21%
SiO2 Content < 1%
Whiteness 92-96%
Mesh Size 100-500 mesh (customizable)
Packaging 25kg bags and Jumbo
  • High brightness and whiteness
  • Excellent thermal stability
  • Low oil absorption
  • Chemically inert
  • Free from heavy metals
  • Consistent particle size distribution
  • Glass manufacturing
  • Ceramic tiles and sanitaryware
  • Steel industry as flux material
  • Paints and coatings
  • Plastic and rubber industries
  • Detergent and soap manufacturing

Coral Wall Putty

Our premium wall putty is formulated with high-quality white cement, polymers, and selected additives to provide excellent adhesion, smooth finish, and long-lasting protection to walls. It is specially designed to fill minor cracks and imperfections, creating a perfect base for paint application.

Base Material White cement based
Coverage 12-15 sq.ft/kg (2 coats)
Drying Time 4-6 hours (depending on conditions)
Particle Size < 90 microns
Packaging 25kg bags
Shelf Life 12 months in original packaging
  • Excellent adhesion to all surfaces
  • Smooth and uniform finish
  • Water resistant
  • Crack bridging capability
  • Low shrinkage
  • Easy to apply and sand
  • Interior and exterior walls
  • Concrete surfaces
  • Plastered surfaces
  • Cement boards
  • As a base for paint applications
  • Repairing minor cracks and imperfections

Coral Stone Crete

Coral stone crete is a single pack, water based coating, based on durable acrylic copolymers and quartz aggregates in aqueous dispersion, Used as a structured decorative coating system for exterior building walls/facades. The thickness of the coating is governed by grade (size) of the quartz aggregates in the plaster. Immediately thereafter the pattern is etched using a plastic trowel. The aggregates provide hardness, resistance to wear and allow the wall to breathe.

Base Material Acrylic copolymer with quartz aggregates
Coverage 4kg/m2, giving a 2 to 2.5mm thickness
Drying Time 4-6 hours (depending on conditions)
Particle Size 1-3mm aggregates
Packaging 25kg bags
Shelf Life 12 months in original packaging
  • Excellent weather resistance
  • Decorative textured finish
  • Water resistant
  • UV stable
  • Breathable coating
  • Easy to apply with trowel
  • Exterior wall facades
  • Decorative wall finishes
  • Commercial buildings
  • Residential exteriors
  • Architectural features
  • Renovation projects
