About Us

Newport Minerals Limited was established in 2020 as a family run business specializing in the manufacturing of ground minerals for industrial and agricultural uses. Over the years NML has grown into a fully-fledged Industry for classified (graded) Calcium Carbonate and Dolomite,ground to varying consistencies and supplied to various manufacturing industries. Newport Minerals Limited is committed to customer satisfaction and has devised a self- certification programme that employs performance measurement.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility utilizes advanced technology to produce consistent, high-performance products that meet international quality standards. We source our raw materials from our own mines, ensuring complete control over quality from extraction to final product.

At Newport Minerals, we are committed to sustainable mining practices and environmentally responsible manufacturing processes. Our team of experts continuously works to innovate and improve our product offerings to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
