Our Story

Newport Minerals Limited was established in 2022 as a family run business specializing in the manufacturing of ground minerals for industrial and agricultural uses. Over the years NML has grown into a fully-fledged Industry for classified (graded) Calcium Carbonate and Dolomite,ground to varying consistencies and supplied to various manufacturing industries. Newport Minerals Limited is committed to customer satisfaction and has devised a self- certification programme that employs performance measurement

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility utilizes advanced technology to produce consistent, high-performance products that meet international quality standards. We source our raw materials from our own mines, ensuring complete control over quality from extraction to final product.

Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner for businesses across various industries. We take pride in our Tanzanian heritage while maintaining global standards in all our operations.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality dolomite and wall putty products that meet the evolving needs of our customers while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility and operational excellence.

Our Vision

To be the preferred supplier of mineral-based construction and industrial materials in East Africa, recognized for our product quality, innovation, and commitment to sustainable development.
